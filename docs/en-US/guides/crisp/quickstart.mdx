# Creating a Project

Let's get started by creating your first project and integrating the Crisp bot!

## Step 1: Sign Up and Log In

Log in via our [Home Page](https://ai.haruka.cloud). You can sign in using third-party accounts such as GitHub or Google, or register a new account with your email. If you choose email registration, please ensure you verify your email address.

> **Note**: If you don't receive the verification email, please check your spam or junk folder.

## Step 2: Create Your Project

Click the **"Create Project"** button in the top right corner of the home page. Give your project a name that is easy to recognize.

**Important Notice:**
Currently, each account is limited to **one project**.

If you require additional projects, please contact us via email at: `support@haruka.cloud`.
